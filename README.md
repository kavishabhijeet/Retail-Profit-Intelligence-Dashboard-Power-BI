# Retail Profit Intelligence Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze retail sales performance, profit margins, and identify areas of profit leakage.

The dashboard helps businesses understand revenue trends, loss-making products, and simulate profit improvement scenarios using pricing, discount, and cost adjustments.

---

## Business Objectives

Analyze revenue and profit performance
Identify loss-making product categories
Understand regional performance differences
Optimize pricing and discount strategy
Improve profitability using scenario simulation

---

## Tools Used

Power BI
Excel dataset
Data cleaning and transformation
Data visualization
KPI design

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Performance Overview

Shows key business metrics:

* Total Revenue
* Net Profit
* Profit Margin
* Average Delivery Days
* Delivery Efficiency %

---

### 2. Profit Leakage & Product Mix Analysis

Identifies:

* Loss-making sub-categories
* Profit margin by category
* Discount impact on profitability
* Regional performance comparison

---

### 3. Profit Optimization Simulator

Interactive simulation to analyze impact of:

* Price change %
* Discount change %
* Cost change %

Helps evaluate strategies to improve profit.

---

## Key Insights

Technology category generates highest revenue
Certain sub-categories contribute to losses
Profit margin varies significantly by region
Discount levels directly impact profitability
Profit can be improved using optimized pricing strategy
